If you look at the iOS health app, and look at the table view for health data you can see a one column table. Each cess contains a UIImageView on the left, a label in the middle, and another UIImageView on the right.

In the maps app there's another one column table. You can search for places and this will display a table, and rapidly alter what it contains. You can see that each cell is repeating in terms of the icon and placement of text.

In contacts, you can see another table view separated into sections. Another example is the stack of images you get when you want to pick an image.

The small repeating views are called cells, or UITableViewCells. Tables draw in the cells vertically and draw in separators.
